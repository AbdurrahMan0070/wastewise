# ⚡ SUPER QUICK GUIDE - Get Your Link in 10 Minutes

## EASIEST WAY: Use Canva Directly

### Step 1: Go to Canva (2 min)
1. Open: canva.com
2. Sign up/Login (free)
3. Click "Create a design"
4. Choose "Presentation"

### Step 2: Create 10 Slides (5 min)
Use `GAMMA_10_SLIDES.txt` as your guide:

**Slide 1:** WasteWise + Your Team Name + Your Names
**Slide 2:** Problem (2 billion tons waste, 50% in landfills)
**Slide 3:** Solution (Scan barcode, get instructions)
**Slide 4:** 6 Features (Scanner, Search, Gamification, Map, Offline, Community)
**Slide 5:** Zero Cost Scaling (Community-powered)
**Slide 6:** Technology (React, Supabase, PWA)
**Slide 7:** Impact (CO₂ tracking, environmental metrics)
**Slide 8:** Why We Win (vs competitors)
**Slide 9:** Screenshots + QR code
**Slide 10:** Try it now + Your Netlify URL

### Step 3: Add Screenshots (2 min)
Take 6 screenshots of your app:
- Windows: Win + Shift + S
- Upload to Canva
- Add to slides

### Step 4: Get Link (1 min)
1. Click "Share" (top right)
2. Click "Link"
3. Set to "Anyone with the link can view"
4. Copy link ✅

**Done! Your link:** `https://www.canva.com/design/xxxxx/view`

---

## ALTERNATIVE: Use Google Slides

1. Go to: slides.google.com
2. Create blank presentation
3. Add your content
4. Share → Anyone with link → Viewer
5. Copy link ✅

**Done! Your link:** `https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/xxxxx/edit`
